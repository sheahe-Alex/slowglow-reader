## 中文版

我做了一个小工具，暂时叫「慢光阅读 Slowglow Reader」。

它是一个本地、无登录、不上传文字的温柔朗读阅读器。你可以把网页、PDF 或文档里的文字复制进去，它会放大显示、朗读出来，并高亮当前读到的位置。

我最想做的是「温柔模式」：它会把当前句托出来，让其他文字轻轻退后，帮助人一小句一小句地读，不用一下子面对整屏文字。

它不是为了逼人读得更快，而是希望阅读变得更不吓人：可以暂停，可以走神后回来，可以只读一点点。

如果你愿意试试看，我很想知道：

- 哪些地方让你舒服？
- 哪些地方让你烦或有压力？
- 你会在什么场景下想用它？

## English

I made a small tool, tentatively called Slowglow Reader.

It is a gentle local read-aloud reader. It does not require login, and it does not upload your text. You can copy text from a webpage, PDF, or document, and it will enlarge the text, read it aloud, and highlight the current word.

The part I care about most is Gentle Mode. It lifts the current sentence forward and lets the surrounding text gently fade back, so you can read one sentence at a time without facing the whole page all at once.

It is not meant to push people to read faster. I want it to make reading feel less scary: you can pause, drift away and come back, or read only a little.

If you are willing to try it, I would love to know:

- What felt comfortable?
- What felt annoying or stressful?
- In what situations would you want to use it?
