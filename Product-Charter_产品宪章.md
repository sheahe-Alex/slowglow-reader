# 伴学系列产品宪章 / Companion Learning Tools Charter

这份宪章记录伴读 Reader 以及未来伴写 Writer、伴研 Researcher 的共同北极星。它不是功能清单，而是提醒我们：这个系列为什么存在，应该保护什么，又应该克制什么。

This charter records the shared north star for Bandu Reader and possible future tools such as Bandu Writer and Bandu Researcher. It is not a feature list. It is a reminder of why this series exists, what it protects, and what it should resist.

## 北极星 / North Star

不替代人的能力，而是为能力生长提供脚手架。  
Do not replace human ability. Build scaffolds that help ability grow.

伴学系列希望陪人把原本属于自己的阅读、思考、研究和表达能力慢慢长出来。  
The Companion Learning Tools series is here to help people slowly grow the reading, thinking, research, and expression abilities that should belong to them.

## 为什么做 / Why This Exists

很多人并不是不愿意深度阅读、严谨写作或认真研究，而是这些过程太容易触发过载、挫败、羞耻和自责。

Many people are not unwilling to read deeply, write carefully, or research seriously. These processes often trigger overload, frustration, shame, and self-blame.

很多教育场景默认学生已经会阅读长文、判断资料、建立概念框架、组织论证和写出清楚表达，但这些能力很少被一步一步教会。于是很多人只能拼凑信息、模仿表达、假装读懂，最后能力没有真正长出来。

Many educational settings assume that students already know how to read long texts, evaluate sources, build conceptual frameworks, organize arguments, and write clearly. These abilities are often not taught step by step. As a result, many people have to patch together information, imitate academic language, and pretend they understood, without actually growing the underlying ability.

伴学系列想做的是补上这块脚手架：让人可以进入文本，留下痕迹，回到重点，把材料变成理解，再把理解长成表达。

This series tries to provide that missing scaffold: helping people enter texts, leave traces, return to key points, turn material into understanding, and turn understanding into expression.

## 我们相信 / Beliefs

- 能力不是被逼出来的，而是在足够安全、足够具体、足够可重复的支持里慢慢长出来的。  
  Ability is not forced into existence. It grows through support that is safe enough, concrete enough, and repeatable enough.
- 阅读困难、写作困难和研究困难不等于没有思考能力。很多时候，它们只是说明脚手架不够。  
  Difficulty with reading, writing, or research does not mean a lack of thinking ability. Often, it means the scaffold is missing.
- 好工具不应该让人更羞耻。它应该让人更容易开始、更容易回来、更容易继续。  
  A good tool should not make people feel more shame. It should make it easier to start, return, and continue.
- AI 可以辅助，但不应该替代人的判断、理解和表达。  
  AI may assist, but it should not replace human judgment, understanding, or expression.
- 深度能力来自反复靠近：第一遍进入，第二遍看清，第 N 遍变成自己的理解。  
  Deep ability comes from repeated return: enter on the first pass, see more clearly on the second, and slowly make the material your own.

## 系列边界 / Product Boundaries

### 伴读 Reader

深度阅读脚手架。  
A scaffold for deep reading.

它帮助人：

- 进入长文和严肃内容
- 减少一次面对整屏文字的压力
- 看见当前句、关键词、关键句和轻量结构
- 留下第二遍、第 N 遍可以回看的痕迹

It helps people:

- enter long-form and serious text
- reduce the pressure of facing the whole page at once
- notice the current sentence, keywords, key sentences, and light structure
- leave traces that can be revisited on later passes

### 伴写 Writer

严谨写作脚手架。  
A scaffold for careful writing.

它未来可以帮助人：

- 从阅读痕迹中整理材料
- 把材料变成自己的问题、观点和论证
- 一段一段写，而不是一开始就面对整篇文章
- 检查空话、证据不足、概念不清和逻辑跳跃

It may later help people:

- organize material from reading traces
- turn material into questions, claims, and arguments
- write paragraph by paragraph instead of facing the whole essay at once
- notice vague claims, weak evidence, unclear concepts, and logic gaps

### 伴研 Researcher

资料调查和研究准备脚手架。  
A scaffold for research preparation and source work.

它未来可以帮助人：

- 搜集资料
- 判断来源质量
- 建立问题意识
- 区分证据、观点、背景和例子

It may later help people:

- gather sources
- evaluate source quality
- build research questions
- distinguish evidence, claims, background, and examples

## 我们不做什么 / What We Avoid

- 不做“替你读完”的总结器。  
  We do not build a summarizer that reads instead of you.
- 不做“替你写完”的作文机器。  
  We do not build a machine that writes the whole piece for you.
- 不把效率放在真实理解之前。  
  We do not put efficiency before real understanding.
- 不把复杂功能堆到一个会让人害怕的界面里。  
  We do not pile complex features into an intimidating interface.
- 不用羞辱、排名、打卡压力或失败惩罚来驱动学习。  
  We do not use shame, ranking, streak pressure, or punishment to drive learning.

## 设计原则 / Design Principles

1. **先降低进入阻力。**  
   Reduce the friction of starting first.

2. **一次只让人面对一小步。**  
   Let people face one small step at a time.

3. **把复杂能力拆成可练习的动作。**  
   Break complex abilities into actions that can be practiced.

4. **保留人的思考痕迹。**  
   Preserve traces of the user's own thinking.

5. **默认本地、低权限、少登录。**  
   Prefer local-first, low-permission, low-login experiences.

6. **把回看设计成一等公民。**  
   Treat returning and reviewing as first-class needs.

7. **温柔不是装饰，而是降低过载的结构。**  
   Gentleness is not decoration. It is a structure that reduces overload.

## 共同语言 / Shared Language

- 进入：开始靠近文本，不被整屏内容吓退。  
  Enter: approach the text without being overwhelmed by the whole page.
- 看见：看见句子、概念、逻辑和重点。  
  Notice: see sentences, concepts, logic, and key points.
- 留下：留下关键词、关键句、摘录、标注和自己的理解。  
  Leave traces: keep keywords, key sentences, excerpts, marks, and one's own understanding.
- 返回：在写作、复习和再次阅读时能重新找到材料。  
  Return: find the material again when writing, reviewing, or rereading.
- 生长：能力在反复、具体、低羞耻的练习中慢慢形成。  
  Grow: ability forms through repeated, concrete, low-shame practice.

## 一句话 / One Sentence

伴学系列陪你真正进入材料，把理解留下来，再把理解长成表达。  
The Companion Learning Tools series helps you truly enter material, keep traces of understanding, and grow that understanding into expression.
